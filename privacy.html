Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or interact with samuelmathai.com (the "Website"). This Website is the official campaign site for Samuel Mathai, candidate for FOMAA Vice President (Term 2026–2028).

The Samuel Mathai for FOMAA VP Campaign is operated and funded by Mathavu, LLC, a legally registered entity responsible for all campaign communications, digital operations, and financial expenditures related to this campaign.

By using this Website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to the collection and use of information as described herein.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you subscribe to campaign updates, express interest in volunteering, attend events, or otherwise communicate with the campaign. This information may include:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (including mobile number)
  • Mailing address
  • Any additional information you choose to provide in messages or forms

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial account details, Social Security numbers, or government-issued identification numbers through this Website.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for the following legitimate campaign purposes:

  • Sending campaign notifications, news, and updates
  • Sharing event invitations, convention schedules, and community gathering details
  • Coordinating volunteer activities and campaign logistics
  • Responding to your inquiries and providing support
  • Improving our campaign communications and outreach

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for commercial purposes.

Mobile Information & SMS Disclosure

When you provide your mobile phone number and opt in to receive text messages (SMS) from the Samuel Mathai for FOMAA VP Campaign, we may send you campaign updates, event reminders, and important notifications via SMS. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Your mobile phone number and SMS opt-in consent are treated with the highest level of confidentiality and are used solely for the campaign communication purposes described in this policy.

SMS Opt-Out & Support

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages from our campaign at any time. To stop receiving text messages, simply reply with the following keyword to any message you receive from us:

STOP HELP
  • Reply "STOP" at any time to opt out of receiving further SMS messages from the campaign.
  • Reply "HELP" at any time for more information about our SMS program or to reach our support team.

After sending "STOP," you will receive a one-time confirmation message verifying that you have been unsubscribed. You will receive no further messages unless you re-subscribe. Opting out of SMS does not affect any other communication preferences (e.g., email).

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
